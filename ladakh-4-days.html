Ladakh Escape: 5 Days of Majestic Landscapes and Cultural Splendor

Union Territory of Ladakh.

Duration

4 Nights 5 days

Group Size

Flexible

Tour Type

Daily Tour

Languages

English, Urdu

Day 1: Arrival in Leh

Morning: Arrive at Leh Kushok Bakula Rimpochee Airport. Meet and greet with our representative at the airport. Transfer to your hotel for check-in and acclimatization. Afternoon: Rest and acclimatize to the high altitude of Leh (11,500 ft). Evening: Take a leisurely walk around Leh Market, explore the local shops and cafés. Return to your hotel for dinner and overnight stay.

Day 2: Sham Valley Excursion

Morning: After breakfast, drive to Sham Valley, also known as the "Apricot Valley." Visit the Magnetic Hill, where vehicles defy gravity. Experience the serenity of the confluence of the Indus and Zanskar rivers at Sangam. Afternoon: Explore Alchi Monastery, famous for its ancient Buddhist murals and architecture. Visit Likir Monastery, home to a giant statue of Maitreya Buddha. Evening: Return to Leh. Enjoy a relaxing evening at your hotel. Dinner and overnight stay in Leh.

Day 3: Local Sightseeing in Leh

Morning: After breakfast, visit the iconic Shanti Stupa, offering panoramic views of Leh and the surrounding mountains. Explore Leh Palace, a historic royal palace overlooking the town. Afternoon: Visit Thiksey Monastery, one of the largest and most impressive monasteries in Ladakh. Explore the Shey Palace and Monastery, the former summer retreat of the Ladakhi kings. Evening: Return to your hotel. Dinner and overnight stay in Leh.

Day 4: Half-Day Excursion to Pangong Lake

Morning: After an early breakfast, drive to Pangong Lake, one of the most breathtakingly beautiful lakes in the world, located about 160 kilometers from Leh. The journey takes you through the Chang La pass (17,590 ft), one of the highest motorable roads in the world. Afternoon: Arrive at Pangong Lake, situated at an elevation of 14,270 ft. Enjoy the mesmerizing views of the lake's crystal-clear blue waters against the backdrop of rugged mountains. Explore the lakeshore and capture stunning photographs. Evening: Return to Leh. Relax and unwind at your hotel. Dinner and overnight stay in Leh.

Day 5: Departure from Leh

Morning: After breakfast, check out from your hotel. Transfer to Leh Kushok Bakula Rimpochee Airport for your departure flight. Bid farewell to the enchanting landscapes and warm hospitality of Ladakh, carrying with you unforgettable memories of this incredible journey.
